Plans and data retention

The app model is simple: keep a local diary, try cloud features for 7 days, then decide whether Premium is useful for your climbing.

Free Local

Diary, projects, sessions and basic analytics work on the phone. Data is not sent to Firebase, and friends, challenges, groups and coach tools are disabled.

Trial Premium

The first app launch gives 7 days of cloud features: Firebase sync, friends, groups, challenges, trainings and coach/team tools.

Premium

Intended as a monthly or yearly Google Play subscription. Premium keeps cloud sync, social features, exports and advanced analytics active.

What happens after Premium ends?

Local data stays on the phone. Data synchronized with Firebase may be retained for up to 12 months after Premium expires so the user can renew the subscription or export data. After that period, cloud data may be deleted.

FAQ

The most common questions are about local storage, Firebase sync, Premium and data deletion.

Does the app work without internet?

Yes. Routes can be saved locally on the phone. If the user has Trial Premium or Premium, offline changes are sent to Firebase when the connection returns.

What happens if I delete a route offline?

Deleting a single route in the app is stored locally as a pending change. When internet returns, the app should synchronize that deletion to Firebase so the route does not come back from the cloud.

Will Firebase restore data after clearing the phone?

If the user clears app data or reinstalls the app, the local database and local pending-change queue are removed. After signing in again with active Trial Premium/Premium, the app can download data that was previously stored in Firebase.

What is the difference between local deletion and account deletion?

Clearing local data removes data only from the phone. Deleting the account and cloud data removes the profile and synchronized Firebase data. This option is available in the app under Profile → Data and privacy.

What does Premium include?

Premium unlocks Firebase sync, friends, groups, challenges, training invitations, coach/team tools, export and advanced features. The first app launch starts a 7-day Trial Premium.
