What is deleted

The request covers Firebase Authentication, profile data, synced climbing data and account-related social data where deletion does not remove records owned by other participants. Local-only data can be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app. Export first if you need a copy.

Subscription is not cancelled automatically

Cancel Premium in Google Play first if you do not want another charge.

Account deletion and subscription cancellation are separate actions.

Timing and exceptions

Requests are normally completed within 30 days. Records required for transactions, security or legal compliance may be retained for the necessary period. See the Privacy Policy.
